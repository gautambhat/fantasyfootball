HOW TO PLAY

IMPORTANT : Use Google Chrome only!

Due to various constraints, this web app has been optimized only for Google Chrome, and running it on any other browser(don't even try Internet Explorer) may cause it to misbehave.

This is the official online fantasy football manager web app for SNU Football League 2013, belonging to the Sports Committee of Shiv Nadar University. Rules, regulations and prizes can be changed without prior notice, and no disputes regarding such shall be entertained. The following is a brief idea of playing Fantasy Football Manager 2013.

  • All the basic pages can be navigated from the bar at the top of each page, as can be seen on this page as well.
  • Users, after logging in, are redirected to the HOME page by default, where they an view the tournament calendar as well as various Fantasy statistics.
  • You can assign your team name and change your password from the ACCOUNT section of the bar.
  • Once teams are locked, the POINTS page will be activated and all the points gained by your team will be displayed there.
  • First time users can pick their team by clicking on the MY TEAM section of the navigation bar, while all subsequent visits to the page displays the user's team as well as giving the user an option to pick his/her team again, provided the teams are not locked during matchday.
  • The user has 100 points to pick a team of seven players (1 Goalie, 2 Defensive players, 4 Offensive Players)
  • A captain, also has to be selected among the 7 players. The captain fetches twice as many points.
  • The points and leaderboard will be frequently updated to reflect performances of players after each matchday. If points of two managers are equal, the team selected with fewer points is ranked higher. So don't feel forced to spend all 100 points!
  • Teams will be locked after matchday 1, and will be reponed for squad overhauling twice- once during the Diwali break, and once after the group stages are concluded.
